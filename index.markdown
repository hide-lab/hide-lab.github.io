## About:
The Hybrid Intelligence and Deception Exploration (HIDE) Lab focuses on understanding the complexity of human-AI interactions and deception in increasingly hybrid societies. The lab is led by <a href="https://www.stefansarkadi.com"> Dr. Stefan Sarkadi</a>, who is currently Associate Professor of AI for Defence and Security at the University of Lincoln. The Lab's aim is to act as an international hub for conducting state-of-the-art AI-based research to tackle the topic of deception.
